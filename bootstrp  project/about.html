Fashion designer skills are the qualifications and knowledge fashion design professionals need to work in the fashion industry. Being a designer requires a varied range of skills as the responsibilities can differ markedly from day to day.

Fashion designer duties include:

  • Sketching designs
  • Selecting fabrics and embellishments and sourcing suppliers
  • Sewing and creating physical pieces

Many designers gain their skills through undergraduate programs in fashion design or fashion merchandising. These programs often require students to learn computer-aided design software and build a design portfolio to show potential employers. You can find accredited post-secondary schools through the National Association of Schools of Art and Design (NASAD).
